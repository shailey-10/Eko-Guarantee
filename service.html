WHY NON-GMO Certification?

Increasing consumer demand.

In today's world customer wants to know what is in their food. With increases awareness for NON-GMO there is a sift increase in demand for certified products (food,textiles, cosmetics etc.)

Companies at all stages of supply chhain starting from farm to supply to manufacture to supply to package have seen huge benifits in terms of increase in sales on NON-GMI verified products.
