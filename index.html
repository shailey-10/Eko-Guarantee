What is GMO?

Genetically modified organisms,is a plant, animal, microorganism or other organisms whose gentic makeup has veen modified in a lab.

Why Non GMO?

NON-GMO Stands for non-genetically modified organsism, an refers to plants, animals or other organisms whose gentic material is changed in ways that do no occur naturally. Brands that achieve NON-GMO roduct verification on their products, provides them leverage over others in the global market.

Impact of GMO?

More than 80% of all GMOs grown worldwide are engineeredfor herbicide tolerance. As a result use of hebicide has increased. Once released in the environment these novel organisms cant be recalled.

How can I avoid GMOS

In the US GMOS are in majorority of onventional processed food. To avoid GMOs, look for the NON-GMO Project verfied label.

CERTIFICATION PROCESS

We work for you !

Application

You submit an initial application fee and site list describing your company products. This information helps EKO-GUARANTEE establish a quote. Send us a signed contract and quote along with your risk assessment fee payment to get started.

Document Submission

You submit detailed information about the processes an products for review. This includes risk assessment,SOPs, production facilities and co packers information.

Document Review

EKO-GUARANTEE technical evaluator reviews the documents you have submitted. We may ask for clarification or more information during the reiews

Any required Corrective action respones eill be reviewed for compliance and a certification decision is made.
